FAQs For Customers

Why Apni Raksha?
It provides all the required information about covid at your fingertips and doesn’t ask you for your personal information to access the predictor.
What is the covid predictor?
The covid predictor asks you some basic questions about your current health status like your vaccination status, symptoms you are currently experiencing like cough, taste, smell, weakness etc and using these parameters it probability of you being infected by covid.
What is the need of covid predictor?
With a lot of cases currently present, people sometimes panic with a little symptoms and think that they might be infected by covid and go for a covid test, which is obviously more dangerous because a lot of already infected people are present there. The predictor just gives a percentage probabilty so that a person can estimate if there is really a need to consult a doctor and get himself tested.
Do I need to give my personal information to use the predictor?
No, you don’t need to put any personal information, you just need to put basic details like vaccination status and answer some simple questions regarding covid symptoms that you are having.
Is the covid predictor completely accurate?
No, it can’t give 100% accurate results, it just gives an estimate of the probability of a person being infected by covid.
Can this predictor replace doctors?
The main goal of our predictor is to provide an intermediary service for the user as he sees fit, this predictor does not aim to replace doctors, any medical facility or any medical institution. We are just a convenient intermediary that guide the individual towards a better and suited medical solution.
